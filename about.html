About

The Southern Mensurationists is an informal group of forest biometricians, mensurationists, statisticians, mathematicians, modelers, and others interested in various aspects of quantitative forestry. The International Conference on Forest Measurements and Quantitative Methods and Management meet periodically (approximately at five-year intervals) in conjunction with a Southern Mensurationists meeting. Past conferences of these two groups have provided a diverse range of presentations given by professionals and students. The level of presentation is often diverse, but we welcome talks designed to be accessible to all, without sacrificing content. Graduate students are especially encouraged to use this venue as a friendly place to get feedback on their proposed research work or work in progress.
