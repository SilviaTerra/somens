The 2016 Joint Northeastern Mensurationists Organization (NEMO) / Southern Mensurationists (SOMENS) annual meeting will be held November 13-15, 2016 at the Colonial Inn in historic Concord Massachusetts. This location is set near Minute Man National Historic Park, Walden Pond, and Ralph Waldo Emerson's house.

The meeting agenda will include a range of invited and solicited talks (in long and short format) and panels, covering a variety of topics including forest sampling, mensuration, modeling, and biometrics. We would specifically welcome abstracts for a themed session on assessing economic efficiency of sampling, measurement, and modelling methods. For those interested in presenting, please send an abstract to: abstracts2016@silviaterra.com

Registration is being administered by the Western Forestry and Conservation Association. Credit cards, checks and purchase orders are accepted. Online registration is available here

The early registration fee is $300 for regular members and $200 for students. After October 15 the registration fee is $350 for regular members and $250 for students.

Guests can call the hotel directly at 978-369-9200 and reference the NEMO Room Block to get the discounted rate or they can book online using a special code. This must be done before 9/18/16 to ensure availability and the discounted rate.

If you would prefer to book your room though the hotel website, click Book Now then in the burgundy date box click on Enter your access or promotional code, select Group Code and enter the code NEMO2016. Then select arrival date and follow the steps to complete the reservation.

Information on presenters will be provided once abstracts have been submitted and approved.
